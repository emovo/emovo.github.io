Welcome to EMOVO!

A worldwide personalized database of retirement move options, all under your fingertips.

Get Started

The Algorithm

Fast and expansive in attaining important data about a place.

Through entering a few pieces of data such as age and monthly income, you can recieve a personalized listing of the best countries, states, or cities for you to live in the future. Approximately 12 factors are assessed per country and 24 factors per US state, in determining the most suitable locations not only in general but specifically related to you. You can also find specific locations through the 'Search' functionality.

US Housing Search Functionality

Fast and expansive in attaining housing data for ~82000 US cities.

Search algorithm is personalized based on your budget. Data is related to preferences regarding renting vs. buying, square footage preference, and state preference.

Wide & Interactive Global Scope

234 countries, 50 states (along with District of Columbia), and a growing list of cities. Comment sections for users to rate locations and give their experience to others.

Data ranging from a variety of societal factors is stored for each of these places, providing you with many options to better make their moving decisions. Data for cities is also ever expanding as more city data is dynamically retrieved from database storage. Comment sections accompany these many locations to help give you not just data but also actual experiences about living in the regions.
